Breaststroke

Breaststroke is often called frog stroke as you legs turn out like a frog when you kick. The arms move in a similar motion at the same time. There are many drills to master the timing of this stroke. 2 kick 1 pull is good to time the kicks with the breathing. Additionally, I Y drill helps for kids to understand the general arm motions.
