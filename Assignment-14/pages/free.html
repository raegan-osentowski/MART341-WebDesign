General Info Butterfly Backstroke Breaststroke Freestyle
Freestyle is the most simple stroke. The stroke encompasses a single arm movement over the head. The stroke envolves a scissor style kick that comes from power of the core and hips. The arms use the back muscles primarly to generate a powerfull pull. Drills are commonly used for kids to learn the strokes fundamentals. Drills can range from learning the pull in the water or the arm recovery.
