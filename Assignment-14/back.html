Backstroke

Backstroke is most younger swimmers favorite stroke as there face does not need to be in the water. Backstroke requires a similar arm motion as freestyle as well as a flutter kick. The stroke is simple for younger kids to understand. The most difficult part of the stroke is keeping your arms in a streamlined position near your head. Additionally, the flipturns and not hitting your head on the wall is incredibily difficult.
