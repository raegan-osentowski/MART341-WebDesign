Ingredients

  • 16 oz chopped spinach
  • 2 bunches parsley
  • 1 large yellow onion, finely chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minched
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 4 eggs
  • 10 oz feta cheese
  • 2 tsp dill weed
  • 16 oz pacakge Phyllo Dough

Instruction

  1. Heat oven to 325 degrees F
  2. Mix filling ingredients
  3. Unroll phyllo sheets and place between two damp kitchen cloths
  4. Prepare a 9.5 by 13 baking dish and brush with olive oil
  5. Add 2 sheets of phyllo dough then brush with olive oil, repeat until 2/3 sheets used
  6. Spread filling over then cover with remaining dough
  7. Fold flaps over the top and brush with olive oil
  8. Bake at 325 for one hour
  9. Remove and enjoy!
